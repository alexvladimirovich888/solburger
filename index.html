ZECT WORKERS FUND

Preserving privacy for those who build after hours.

We represent individuals who live two lives. By day — professionals, employees, and builders. By night — traders, launchers, and creators of the absurd. We believe that anonymity is a form of safety. It protects freedom, creativity, and chaos — the roots of innovation.

How the Fund Operates

The ZECT Workers Fund operates through a decentralized network of anonymous contributions and mutual protection protocols. Every donation directly supports the maintenance of digital privacy infrastructure and protective measures for our members. We exist as a collective of individuals who understand that creative freedom requires the right to operate without exposure or professional consequence. Through anonymous channels and encrypted communications, we provide resources, legal support, and technological protection to those who build, create, and innovate in spaces that demand discretion.
